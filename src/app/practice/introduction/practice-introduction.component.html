Practice makes perfect!
Here you can find various types of exercises involving the Karnaugh map. Simply choose any category you want to practise.

Label Squares
Assign each state in the Karnaugh map with its corresponding decimal number.
Expression to Karnaugh Map
Mark all the states in the Karnaugh map which either satisfy or are excluded by the given expression. This is the first step you need to do when minimizing the expression or turning it into DNF or CNF form.
Find the Best Groups
Given the Karnaugh map with marked states, group them in the best possible way. For the guidance about grouping states in the Karnaugh map, see tutorial
Name teh Group
For a given group marked in the Karnaugh Map, indicate the values of all variables whose conjunction uniquely identify the group.
Karnaugh Map to Expression
For the given Karnaugh Map, group the marked states in the best possible way and then find the expression uniquely identifying each of them to produce the expression in DNF describing the initial Karnaugh map.
Minimize the Expression
For the given expression, find its minimal DNF or CNF going through the Karnaugh map procedure step by step.
